Listing Distribution

by Lena
4 November 2019

Reading time: 1 minute

What are the benefits of updated listings?


By taking advantage of our listing services, you’ll see real results within a few weeks that will have a consistently positive effect on your business. The task of listing your site, services, location and more is vital if you want to generate more sales.

Having your listings protected and updated is very important to your visibility. We help by distributing your correct business information to a multitude of the most popular search engines, directories, sites, and GPS services in order to make sure your data is in compliance, and more importantly, can be easily found.

Once the job is done right, you will be in the best position to attract potential customers, who may become paying customers that’ll ultimately refer you to a friend, online or in person. Probably online.


Why is a strong presence important for local businesses?


A strong online presence is the first step toward success. With Premium Visibility, your business receives the most essential thing necessary to start building your online reputation: the ability to be easily, organically found by potential customers.



Why is it important for local businesses to have a mobile-optimized listing?


For SEO, priority is primarily given to good customer experience on mobile devices. Consumers will use mobile devices to find desired goods or services and will generally begin these searches with a category or brand search. In the case of a category search, the user has the intent to purchase something in a category but has not decided or does not care about the brand. In most cases, it will be a category search which is heavily skewed toward proximity and location, for example, “martial arts school in Williamsburg”. A brand search would be “Bank of America in Williamsburg”. So search engines generally deliver the closest locations and it is therefore important that location info is crawlable (displayed on your website or page) and is found in meta-data and keywords.



Where are these listings found? Why is it important to have them updated?


We can help you quickly establish accurate business listings on more than 70 reputable business listing sites in the U.S., including Apple Maps, Google, Yahoo!, Bing Maps, and HERE Maps.


Smaller, local listing sites are often overlooked, but just as important for online visibility. When your listing is accurate across the board, it not only makes your business easier to find by default, it also boosts your ability to be found on more well-known platforms such as Google Maps.
